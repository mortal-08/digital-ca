# Digital CA Platform — Features

Your all-in-one platform for managing finances, taxes, and compliance.

---

## 🔐 Account & Login
- Create an account or sign in securely
- Password protection with visibility toggle
- Admin and Client user roles
- Logout anytime from the sidebar

---

## 📊 Dashboard
- Personalized greeting based on time of day
- Overview of total clients, active returns, pending tasks, and revenue
- Quick action buttons for common tasks
- Revenue trend chart for the current financial year
- Service breakdown chart
- Recent activity feed
- Upcoming tax and compliance deadlines with urgency alerts

---

## 🧮 Financial Calculators
- **Income Tax** — Compare Old vs New regime, see slab-wise breakdown
- **GST** — Calculate inclusive or exclusive GST at any rate
- **EMI** — Find monthly EMI, total interest, and total payment for loans
- **TDS Rates** — Look up TDS rates by section (194A, 194C, 194H, etc.)
- **HRA Exemption** — Calculate HRA exemption under Section 10(13A)

---

## 📄 Document Upload & Storage
- Drag and drop files to upload (PDF, Excel, Images)
- Files stored securely on the cloud (Cloudinary)
- Organize documents by category — Tax Returns, Invoices, Reports, Audit, KYC
- Search and filter your documents
- Download or delete anytime

---

## 📈 Reports & Analytics
- **Business Overview** — Monthly revenue, expenses, and profit charts
- **Tax Breakdown** — Visual split of Income Tax, GST, TDS, and more
- **Service Performance** — Radar chart comparing revenue, satisfaction, and efficiency
- **India Economic Trends** — Inflation, RBI Repo Rate, and GDP Growth (2018–2026)
- **Tax Collection Trends** — Direct vs Indirect tax data across financial years
- **Compliance Score** — Track filing status for ITR, GST, TDS, Audit, and ROC

---

## 📡 Live News & Updates
- Real-time news from official government sources:
  - Reserve Bank of India (RBI)
  - Securities and Exchange Board (SEBI)
  - Income Tax Department
  - Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA)
- Filter news by source
- See which articles are live vs cached
- Refresh button to pull latest updates

---

## ✅ Task Management
- Create and assign tasks with deadlines
- Set priority — Low, Medium, High, Urgent
- Track status — Pending, In Progress, Completed
- Filter tasks by status or priority
- Overdue warnings for missed deadlines

---

## 💬 Messages
- Send queries and messages through the platform
- Subject and message fields for clear communication

---

## ⚙️ Settings
- **Profile** — Update your name, phone number, and firm name
- **Security** — Change your password with confirmation
- **Notifications** — Toggle email alerts, deadline reminders, news updates, and task notifications
- **Appearance** — Switch between Light, Dark, or System theme

---

## 📱 Works Everywhere
- Fully responsive — works on desktop, tablet, and mobile
- Smooth animations and modern design
- Dark and light mode support
